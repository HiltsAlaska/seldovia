**MCFAY DOLCHUCK** (Matfoy), the son of Ivan and Elena Dolchuck, was born on August 22, 1875 in Seldovia, Alaska. He died in Seldovia on June 24, 1939. He is buried in the Seldovia City Cemetery in Plot #88.
